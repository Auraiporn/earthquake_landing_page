"An earthquake can happen anywhere. However, the vast majority of earthquakes occur at the boundaries between tectonic plates. Continental and oceanic plates may move toward each other, scrape past each other, or pull apart as they move slowly across the planet's upper mantle. This movement of the plates, and the pressure that is built up at the boundaries, can result in earthquakes" (National Geografic).
